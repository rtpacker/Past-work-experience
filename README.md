## Featured Projects

### 🔬 Multi-Institutional EHR Pipeline (MITH)
Centralized data pipeline integrating EHR data across six hospital systems — admissions, labs, medications, and diagnostic codes — producing a unified analytic dataset for large-scale thrombosis research.
- Modular R workflows using `data.table` and `dplyr`
- Standardized clinical definitions across institutions
- Time-indexed exposure feature engineering for causal modeling

📄 [Published in J. Thrombosis & Haemostasis](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jtha.2023.10.015)

---

### 💊 Heparin-Induced Thrombocytopenia (HIT) Detection
Scalable cohort identification framework combining lab results, medication administration records, and diagnostic codes to detect high-risk adverse drug events.
- Rolling exposure window feature engineering
- Audit-ready validation for cross-institutional reproducibility

---

### 🧬 Clonal Hematopoiesis & Stroke Risk (REGARDS)
Genomic risk modeling using a national cohort of 30,000+ participants to stratify stroke risk using high-dimensional clinical and genomic features.
- Cox proportional hazards survival models
- Complex survey design adjustment
- Interaction analysis of genomic markers and demographic factors
